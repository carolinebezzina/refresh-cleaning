ABOUT US

Since 2008 my company has been cleaning Data Centres, computer rooms and offices in secure facilities. Firstly as a contractor to a national I.T. cleaning company, we gained several years’ valuable experience cleaning Data Centres, in Sydney, Brisbane, Gold Coast and Queensland areas.

 

During that time we have done cleaning work in Commonwealth Bank, Vodafone, Macquarie Bank, Virgin Blue, Network 10, Channel 7, Ernst and Young, University and Government facilities, just to name a few. As a company we are very aware of the critical nature and duty of care required for cleaning the I.T. environment.
