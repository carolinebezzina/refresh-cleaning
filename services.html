SERVICES

 

Create a regular cleaning maintenance program with us. We will track the cleaning and ensure you are reminded, taking the worry off you.

 

Data Centre Cleaning:

 

Sub-Floor Cleaning

6 or 12 month maintenance cleaning: Includes lifting of sub-floor with thorough vacuum and removal of contamination, rubbish, dust and particles. Followed by a complete Above Floor clean.

 

For new Data Centres and rooms:
Builders Cleans during construction and an Initial Clean at completion of server and equipment installation.

 

Above Floor Cleaning

For: server rooms, computer rooms, switch rooms, plant rooms, communications cupboards, PABX rooms, ancillary rooms

Includes floor vacuum, dusting of cabinets, remove marks from cabinets and walls, mop floor. You can nominate whether you want inside the cabinet doors or other cleaning done.

Can be done monthly, quarterly, periodically

 

Office and Facilities

You can nominate the level of service you require. Anything from a weekly or daily clean of your offices and facilities to a complete service covering all your cleaning needs. This allows you to focus on your core business while we will find the best cleaning solutions for keeping your business looking good at all times. Or we can clean specially the offices, facilities and areas you require.

 

The highest Duty of Care is exercised inside Data Centres with our specialised equipment and work procedures. References can be provided
